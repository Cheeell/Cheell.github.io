Cookie Policy

Effective date: January 20, 2025

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us provide, protect, and improve our services.

How We Use Cookies

  • Essential Cookies — Required for basic functionality, authentication, and security.
  • Analytics Cookies — Help us understand usage and improve performance (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Functionality Cookies — Remember your preferences (e.g., language, saved settings).
  • Marketing Cookies — Enable advertising features on platforms like Meta and Google.

Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party providers that set cookies (e.g., Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, LinkedIn Insight Tag, Stripe). These providers have their own privacy and cookie policies.

Your Choices

On first visit, we show a cookie banner to obtain consent for non-essential cookies. You can:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Reject non-essential cookies
  • Manage preferences by category

You can also manage cookies through your browser settings at any time.

Managing Cookies in Your Browser

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies & site permissions

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here with a new effective date.
